# Getting High-Resolution Company Logos

The current logos are low-res. Here are the best sources for high-quality versions:

## Official Brand Resources (BEST QUALITY):

### 1. **Deloitte**
- URL: https://www2.deloitte.com/us/en/pages/about-deloitte/articles/about-deloitte.html
- Or search: "Deloitte brand guidelines" or "Deloitte press kit"
- Look for: White or black version on transparent background
- Recommended: SVG or PNG @ 2x resolution

### 2. **Volkswagen**
- URL: https://www.volkswagen-newsroom.com/en/volkswagen-brand-11
- Media Center usually has logos
- Look for: Circle VW logo, transparent PNG or SVG
- Recommended size: 500px+ width

### 3. **Audi**
- URL: https://www.audi.com/en/company/brand.html
- Audi Media Center
- Look for: Four rings logo, transparent background
- Recommended: SVG preferred

### 4. **Commerzbank**
- URL: https://www.commerzbank.de/en/hauptnavigation/presse/index.html
- Press section usually has logo downloads
- Look for: Logo with transparent background

### 5. **CARIAD**
- URL: https://cariad.technology/en/media.html
- Look for: Media/Press section
- Logo should be high-res PNG or SVG

### 6. **Google Cloud**
- URL: https://cloud.google.com/brand-guidelines
- Official brand guidelines with downloadable assets
- Get the "Google Cloud" horizontal lockup logo
- Recommended: SVG or PNG @ 2x

## Quick Alternative Sources:

### Brandfetch (Excellent quality)
Visit: https://brandfetch.com
Search for each company name
Download PNG or SVG in highest resolution

### Seeklogo
Visit: https://seeklogo.com
Search and download in vector format (SVG) when possible

### WorldVectorLogo
Visit: https://worldvectorlogo.com
Download SVG versions for perfect clarity

## What to Download:

- **Format**: SVG (best) or PNG at 2x resolution (1000px+ width)
- **Background**: Transparent
- **Color**: Original colors OR white version for dark backgrounds
- **File naming**: Keep the same names:
  - `deloitte.png` or `deloitte.svg`
  - `volkswagen.png` or `volkswagen.svg`
  - `audi.png` or `audi.svg`
  - `commerzbank.png` or `commerzbank.svg`
  - `cariad.png` or `cariad.svg`
  - `google-cloud.png` or `google-cloud.svg`

## After Downloading:

Place the new high-res logos in:
```
public/logos/
```

Replace the existing files. The website will automatically use the new ones!

# Company Logos

Place the following company logo PNG files in this directory:

## Required Files:

1. **deloitte.png** - Deloitte logo (white/transparent background)
2. **volkswagen.png** - Volkswagen logo
3. **audi.png** - Audi four rings logo
4. **commerzbank.png** - Commerzbank logo
5. **cariad.png** - CARIAD logo
6. **google-cloud.png** - Google Cloud logo

## Where to Get Official Logos:

### Quick Option - Brand Guidelines / Press Kits:
- **Deloitte**: Search "Deloitte logo transparent PNG" or use their brand center
- **Volkswagen**: VW Media Center or brand portal
- **Audi**: Audi Media Center
- **Commerzbank**: Commerzbank Press & Media
- **CARIAD**: CARIAD Media Center / Press Kit
- **Google Cloud**: [Google Cloud Brand Resources](https://cloud.google.com/press)

### Alternative Sources:
- **Clearbit Logo API**: `https://logo.clearbit.com/{domain}` (e.g., `logo.clearbit.com/deloitte.com`)
- **Brandfetch**: Search for company logos at brandfetch.com
- **WorldVectorLogo**: worldvectorlogo.com (SVG versions available)
- **LogosWorld**: logosworld.net

## Specifications:

- **Format**: PNG with transparent background
- **Color**: White or brand color (will adapt to dark theme)
- **Resolution**: ~300-500px wide recommended
- **Aspect Ratio**: Maintain original logo proportions

## Tips:

For dark backgrounds (your site), get:
- White/light colored versions
- OR logos with transparent backgrounds that work on dark

Some companies provide "inverse" or "white" versions specifically for dark backgrounds.
